# Uniform Space Sampler

Sampler that draws uniformly from the search space or uniformly without
replacement from a given pool.

Creates a new uniform space sampler.

## Format

[R6::R6Class](https://r6.r-lib.org/reference/R6Class.html) object
inheriting from
[SpaceSampler](https://celecx.mlr-org.com/reference/SpaceSampler.md).

## Construction

    clx_sps("uniform")
